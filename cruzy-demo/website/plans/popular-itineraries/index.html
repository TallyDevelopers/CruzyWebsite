How to Find Your Perfect Voyage

Carnival's cruise itineraries offer exciting experiences for every type of person, from the fun-loving adventurer to the poolside lounger. But so many great options can make choosing the right one for you a challenge. Consider your cruise's:

  • Destination(s): The destination is one of the most important parts of your choice. Choose a place to visit that intrigues you and offers activities that you and your fellow travelers will enjoy.
  • Length: Cruise routes range from less than a week to more than a month. How much time you want to spend at sea can help you narrow down your options.
  • Timing: While some destinations may have beautiful weather year-round, others can provide vastly different experiences depending on the season you travel there. Consider what parts of your destination you are most excited about and make sure your cruise falls at a favorable time of year.
  • Amenities: Not every cruise ship is created equal. Some value family-friendly fun and encourage cruisers of all ages. Others cater to a more adult clientele or even have themes to appeal to certain demographics. Whether you're going solo, on an intimate couples' retreat or bringing the entire family, choose a cruise that fits your vibes to make the experience everything you want it to be.
