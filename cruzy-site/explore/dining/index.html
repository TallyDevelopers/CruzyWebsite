Dining

Prepare your taste buds for a culinary journey like no other! Our cruises offer a wide range of dining experiences to satisfy every craving and delight every palate. From the traditional elegance of our general dining room to the flavors of Italy at Cucina del Capitano, every meal is an adventure. Celebrate with the little ones at the whimsical Thing 1 & Thing 2 Birthday Breakfast or indulge in mouthwatering BBQ at Guy's Pig & Anchor. For an intimate and exclusive experience, reserve a seat at The Chef's Table. Craving something more exotic? Chibang offers a fusion of flavors, while JiJi Asian Kitchen serves up vibrant Asian delicacies. Whether you're enjoying a leisurely Seaday Brunch, grabbing fresh seafood from the Seafood Shack, or diving into a cheesy slice from Pizza Pirate, we've got every taste covered. Don’t forget to treat yourself to a sweet delight from Swirls or savor the perfect cut of steak from our Steakhouse Selections. No matter where you choose to dine, every meal on board is an experience to remember.
